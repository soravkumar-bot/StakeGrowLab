Care Tips

Plant Care Essentials

Master the fundamentals and your plants will reward you with lush, healthy growth.

💧

Watering

Check the top inch of soil before watering. Most plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Use room-temperature water when possible.

Learn More →
☀️

Light Requirements

Full sun means 6+ hours, partial shade means 3–6 hours, and low light means less than 3 hours of direct sunlight per day.

Learn More →
🌿

Fertilizing

Feed during the active growing season (spring and summer). Use a balanced NPK fertilizer every 2–4 weeks or slow-release granules every 3 months.

Learn More →
✂️

Pruning & Repotting

Prune dead or yellowing leaves to redirect energy. Repot when roots outgrow the container — usually every 1–2 years in spring.

Learn More →
🐛

Pest Control

Check the undersides of leaves regularly. Neem oil, insecticidal soap, and sticky traps handle most common houseplant pests safely.

Learn More →
🌡️

Temperature & Humidity

Most tropicals thrive between 60–80°F. Grouping plants together naturally raises humidity — or use a small humidifier nearby.

Learn More →
