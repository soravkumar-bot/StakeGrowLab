FAQs

Common Questions

Quick answers to the things we're asked most often.

How often should I water my houseplants?

It depends on the plant and conditions, but a good rule of thumb is to check the top inch of soil. If it's dry, water. If it's still damp, wait another day or two.

What's the easiest plant for a complete beginner?

We always recommend a Snake Plant or Golden Pothos. Both tolerate low light, inconsistent watering, and still look great.

Can I grow plants if I have very little sunlight?

Absolutely. Many plants — ZZ plants, Peace Lilies, and Pothos — actually prefer indirect or low light, making them perfect for darker rooms.

Why are my plant's leaves turning yellow?

Yellowing leaves are usually caused by overwatering, poor drainage, or a lack of nutrients. Check your watering schedule and ensure the pot has drainage holes.
